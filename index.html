About Us

Located in Burbank, CA, Underpass Studios strives to make professional recording available to everyone. Aside from our "Packages," Underpass Studios also offers services in:

  • Film and TV scoring
  • Sound design
  • Demo recording
  • Drum production
  • Programming drums
  • String arranging and recording
  • Podcasts
  • And more!

For non-packaged services, we are flexible and committed to working within your budget. We can't wait to bring your next project to life!
